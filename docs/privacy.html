NoIReject — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2026

NoIReject (the “app”) is a personal mood-and-moments journal. This page explains what we collect, how it’s stored, and the choices you have.

What we collect

  • Email address — used only to identify your account and let you sign in.
  • Account content you create: your moments (type, intensity, tags, optional note, date), your custom tags, and your goals / “what helps me” entries.
  • Authentication tokens — stored on your device so you stay signed in. If you sign in with Apple, we receive only a stable user identifier and (if you choose to share it) your email; we never receive your Apple ID password.

We do not collect analytics, advertising identifiers, location, contacts, photos, microphone, or any data outside the app. We do not run third-party trackers.

How it’s stored

Your account and content are stored in our database hosted on Supabase (operated by Supabase, Inc. on AWS infrastructure). Data is transmitted over HTTPS and is only readable by you when signed in (Supabase Row Level Security restricts every row to its owner). A small encrypted copy is cached on your device so the app works offline.

Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third party for marketing or advertising. Supabase processes data on our behalf as our infrastructure provider.

Your choices

  • Export. Use our contact form to request a copy of your data.
  • Delete your account. In the app, open the profile menu → Delete Account. This removes your account, all moments, custom tags, and focus entries. The action is immediate and irreversible.
  • Sign out at any time from the same menu.

Children

NoIReject is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

If we change this policy we’ll update the date above and, for material changes, ask you to re-acknowledge the policy on next sign-in.

Contact

Questions? Send us a note via the contact form.
